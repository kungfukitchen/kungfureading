Restaurant manager

Job description

  • Responsibility for the day-to-day operations of our restaurant
  • Ensure health and safety legislation is being met
  • Manage stock and wastage levels
  • Overall financial budget & P&L responsibility
  • Working with the directors and accounts team to achieve targets
  • Managing labour costs and staff schedules
  • >Responsible for customer experience, ensuring consistent customer service is being delivered at all times
  • Dealing with customers' complaints

Skills, experience, and qualifications required

  • Previous restaurant manager experience is a must
  • Preferred experience in Chinese food restaurant
  • Excellent interpersonal and problem-solving abilities
  • Excellent customer service skills
  • A true team leader with exceptional management skills
  • Ability to demonstrate accounting knowledge
  • Strong knowledge of Health & Safety, EHO Standards and COVID-19 awareness
  • Chinese language is preferred

Salary: £22k to £26k per year depending on experience

Please send all applications to us by email before 15 November 2022. Thank you, and good luck!
